# Xiaochuan Li

**Causal NLP · Mechanism Shift · Stable Inference · Human & AI**

I am interested in how language models preserve or fail to preserve stable reasoning mechanisms under perturbation, distribution shift, and intervention.

My current research focuses on:

- Stable inference under perturbation
- Shortcut learning and reasoning failure
- Mechanism shift in language models
- Controlled reasoning environments
- Causal Inferential Yield (CIY)
